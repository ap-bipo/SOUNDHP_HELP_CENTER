Voice Notes & Recording

1. How to Record a Voice Note

SoundHP is a voice-first social network. Recording your thoughts is simple and fast:

  • Open the SoundHP app and navigate to the Recording screen (center waveform icon in the navigation bar).
  • Tap the large circular Record button. A pulsing indicator will show that your microphone is actively recording.
  • Tap the button again to stop recording. Each voice note can be between 1 and 60 seconds long.

2. Reviewing Your Recording

Once stopped, SoundHP generates a visual audio waveform of your recording. You can tap play/pause or drag along the minimap to preview your audio before sharing.

3. Adding Captions & Post Visibility

Before publishing, you can type a text caption in the "Write something..." field. On the right panel, select your post visibility:

  • Connected (Default): Shared with all your accepted connections on their SoundHP voice feed.
  • Specific Connections: Tap individual user profile avatars to restrict the post visibility to selected friends.
Pro Tip: Tap the trash icon on the left of the record button at any time to discard the current draft and start fresh.

Profile & Customization

1. Managing Profile Information

Your profile represents your identity on SoundHP. Tap the Profile tab in the bottom bar to view:

  • Avatar & Cover Photo: Tap Edit Profile to upload new photos from your device library.
  • Display Name: The name shown at the top of your posts and in chat lists.
  • Unique Handle (@user_id): Your distinct username used for search and mentions.
  • Statistics: Quick counters showing your total voice posts and active connections.

2. Post History

All your published voice notes are stored on your profile page in chronological order. Tapping any post card opens the full player view where you can re-listen or delete your post.

Connections & Friends

1. Searching for Users

Tap the Search icon on your profile page to search for friends. You can search by entering either their Display Name or unique `@user_id`. Real-time debounced search returns accurate user matches instantly.

2. Connection Request Flow

  • Sending Requests: Tap the Connect button on any user's profile card.
  • Accepting Requests: Tap the Bell icon (Notifications) on your profile to view incoming requests. Tap Accept to establish a mutual connection.
Mutual Privacy: SoundHP requires mutual connections before users can view private voice posts or initiate direct voice chats.

Messaging & Voice Chat

1. Real-Time Private Voice Chat

SoundHP direct messaging is voice-first. Navigate to the Messages tab to view your active conversation rooms with connected friends.

2. Audio Auto-Play & Real-Time Sync

  • Hold or tap the mic button in a chat to record and send a quick voice message.
  • Incoming messages stream in real-time. When actively viewing a chat room, incoming voice messages auto-play seamlessly.
  • Time group headers automatically group chat logs into clean sessions (spaced by 20+ minute gaps).

Privacy, Safety & Account Deletion

1. Security & Data Protection

SoundHP uses Supabase as its core cloud infrastructure. Row-Level Security (RLS) policies guarantee that your private recordings, chat logs, and profile info are accessible strictly to authorized connections.

2. Account & Data Deletion Instructions (App Store Guideline 5.1.1)

In accordance with Apple App Store policies, you have full ownership of your data and can delete your account at any time:

In-App Deletion Steps:

  • Open SoundHP and tap the Profile tab.
  • Tap the Settings (Gear icon) at the top right.
  • Select Delete Account.
  • Confirm the prompt by typing DELETE in capital letters and tap Confirm Account Deletion.

Requesting Web Deletion:

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing our support team directly at soundhp.team@gmail.com from your registered email address with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

Permanent Action: Account deletion permanently purges all your profile data, voice note recordings, audio storage files, chat messages, and connection history. This process is irreversible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick solutions to common queries

Why isn't my microphone recording audio?

add
Make sure SoundHP has microphone permissions enabled on your smartphone. On iOS, go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone > SoundHP and switch the toggle to ON. On Android, go to Settings > Apps > SoundHP > Permissions > Microphone > Allow.

How long can my voice posts be?

add
Voice posts can be up to 60 seconds long. This keeps your feed concise, engaging, and easy to listen to on the go.

I didn't receive my 6-digit email OTP code

add
First, check your email Spam/Junk folder. Ensure your email address was typed correctly. There is a mandatory 60-second cooldown timer between OTP resend attempts to prevent email rate limiting.

Is SoundHP free to use?

add
Yes! SoundHP is 100% free for all users with no hidden fees or subscriptions required for recording, connecting, or voice messaging.

How do I contact the support team?

add
You can visit our Report Bug page or send an email directly to soundhp.team@gmail.com. We respond to support inquiries within 24 hours.
